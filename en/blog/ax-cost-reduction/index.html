3. Proven Case Study — T-CAG Platform: ¥55.1B Annual Savings at Busan Port

This is not theory — it's real results. Handaro AI's flagship research, the T-CAG (Terminal Container AI Guidance) Platform, applied prescriptive AI to container handling operations at Busan Port.

T-CAG Platform Key Results

32.4%
Container reprocessing rate reduction
¥55.1B

Real-time
On-site AI command prescription

T-CAG Platform does not merely track container positions. The AI analyzes all variables on-site in real time (weather, vessel schedules, equipment status, workforce) and directly issues commands on the optimal sequence and route for each container.

This is the difference between Predictive AI and Prescriptive AI. Predictive AI says, "Cargo volume will increase tomorrow." Prescriptive AI commands, "Move Crane 3 to Zone D right now, and process Container 7 first."
