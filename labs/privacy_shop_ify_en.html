Shopify Apps Privacy Policy

MaeBara Labs

Last Updated: December 29, 2025

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy applies to all applications and services provided by Maebara Labs for the Shopify platform. We are committed to protecting the privacy of Shopify merchants and their store data.

2. Data We Collect

To provide our Services, we collect and process only the minimum necessary data from your Shopify store, which may include:

  • Store Information: Shop name, email, and domain.
  • Product Data: Titles, descriptions, and metadata required for AI optimization (e.g., for the Pure LLM app).
  • Usage Data: Non-identifiable statistics such as AI agent visit logs to provide analytics.

3. How We Use Data

We use the collected information solely to:

  • Operate and maintain the functionality of our apps.
  • Generate specialized files (like llms.txt) for AI agents.
  • Provide analytics dashboards for merchants to monitor AI traffic.

Important: We never sell your data to third parties or use it for unauthorized advertising.

4. Data Retention and Security

We retain your data only as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with our Services. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access.

5. Third-Party Services

Our Services interact with:

  • Shopify API: To access store data and provide app functionality
  • AI Agents: To facilitate discovery by third-party AI agents (e.g., OpenAI, Anthropic)

These third parties have their own privacy policies regarding how they crawl the web and process data.
